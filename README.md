# Employee Attrition Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

Employee attrition can significantly impact workforce stability, productivity, and hiring costs. This project analyzes employee attrition data to identify trends across departments, job roles, overtime patterns, and employee demographics using Tableau and SQL.

## Business Objective

The goal of this analysis is to understand factors associated with employee turnover and provide insights that can support workforce planning and retention strategies.

## Tools & Technologies

* Tableau Public
* SQL
* Microsoft Excel

## Dashboard Highlights

* Department-wise attrition analysis
* Job role attrition comparison
* Overtime impact on attrition
* Age group distribution of attrition

## Key Findings

* Research & Development recorded the highest attrition among departments.
* Laboratory Technicians and Sales Executives showed the highest attrition among job roles.
* Employees working overtime experienced higher attrition than employees who did not work overtime.
* Attrition was concentrated among early and mid-career employees.
